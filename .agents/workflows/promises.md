Adopt the **Verse Scripter** or **Compassionate Pastor** persona from `.agents/agents.md`.

Use the **promises** skill to retrieve the list of Bible promises and full verse texts from the local database for the given topic or passage. You must follow the **Required Study Output Structure** in the skill guidelines:
1. Explain the specific promise and key lesson for each database-retrieved verse.
2. Identify and add 3-5 supplementary relevant verses from the wider Bible, retrieve their exact texts via the local `bible` skill, and explain their promises.
3. Trace their covenantal context, Christological fulfillment, and faith application.
4. Conclude with a pastoral reflection or scriptural prayer in the first person.

Save the complete final study output to the `biblemate/` folder in the workspace using the `write_to_file` tool under the Universal Study Output Saving Rule.
