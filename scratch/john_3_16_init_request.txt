Study of John 3:16. Conduct an in-depth, academically rigorous and pastorally rich exegetical study of John 3:16. This study must retrieve and compare multiple English translations (such as NET, KJV, ASV, ESV2016, and NIV), explore the Greek original text (OHGB), analyze key Greek words (including agapao, kosmos, monogenes, pisteuo, aionios), integrate historical and cultural context (the conversation between Jesus and Nicodemus in John 3), pull scholarly observations from classic commentaries, trace cross-references, synthesize major theological themes (Soteriology, Christology, eternal life vs. perishing), and construct practical, heart-warming devotional applications, small group questions, and a scriptural prayer.
