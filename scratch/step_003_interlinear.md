## Stylistic English Re-translation

Combining the precise lexical values with standard English syntax yields a literal study translation that respects the grammatical priorities of the Koine Greek:

> *"For in this manner God loved the world: He gave His unique, one-of-a-kind Son, in order that everyone continuously believing in Him might not perish, but instead continually possess eternal life."*
